    How To Serve

    An exciting, artsy way to enjoy coffee. Delve into more drip coffee experiences with us.

    1. Unpack, then tear off the top of the filter bag to reveal the coffee inside.
    2. Carefully retract the paper "handles" gently, then hook them over the sides of the glass.
    3. Slowly pour 150–200ml of boiling water, allowing the coffee to steadily brew and drain through.
    4. Dispose of the used filter bag in its foil sachet. Enjoy your real cup of fresh coffee!
